# How to Divide Fractions

This guide shows you how to divide any two fractions or mixed numbers.

## Prerequisites

You can multiply fractions and convert between mixed numbers and improper fractions.

## Steps

1. **If either value is a mixed number, convert to an improper fraction.**

    $2\frac{1}{3} \to \frac{7}{3}$

2. **If the divisor is a whole number, write it as a fraction.**

    $5 \to \frac{5}{1}$

3. **Take the reciprocal of the divisor** (swap its numerator and denominator).

    $$\frac{4}{5} \to \frac{5}{4}$$

4. **Multiply the dividend by the reciprocal.**

    $$\frac{7}{3} \div \frac{4}{5} = \frac{7}{3} \times \frac{5}{4} = \frac{35}{12}$$

5. **Simplify and convert to a mixed number if desired.**

    $$\frac{35}{12} = 2\frac{11}{12}$$

## Dividing by a whole number

$$\frac{3}{4} \div 2 = \frac{3}{4} \times \frac{1}{2} = \frac{3}{8}$$

## Dividing a whole number by a fraction

$$6 \div \frac{3}{4} = \frac{6}{1} \times \frac{4}{3} = \frac{24}{3} = 8$$
