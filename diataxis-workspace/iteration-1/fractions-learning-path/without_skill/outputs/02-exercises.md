## Section I: Word Problems

1. A recipe calls for 2/3 cup of flour. You want to make 1 1/2 times the recipe.
   How much flour do you need?

2. A board is 5 1/4 feet long. You cut off a piece that is 1 7/8 feet long.
   How long is the remaining piece?

3. You drove 3/4 of the total distance to your destination in the morning, and
   1/6 of the total distance after lunch. What fraction of the trip remains?

4. A tank is 2/5 full. After adding 15 liters, it is 3/4 full. What is the total
   capacity of the tank?

5. Three friends split a restaurant bill. The first pays 1/3, the second pays 2/5.
   What fraction does the third friend pay?

6. A stock price dropped by 1/8 of its value on Monday, then rose by 1/5 of its
   new value on Tuesday. If it started at $120, what is it worth after Tuesday?
