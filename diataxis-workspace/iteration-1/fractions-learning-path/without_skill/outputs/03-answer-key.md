## Section C: Comparing Fractions

1. 3/5 vs. 4/7: Cross multiply: 3*7=21, 5*4=20. 21 > 20, so **3/5 > 4/7**
2. 5/8 vs. 7/12: Cross multiply: 5*12=60, 8*7=56. 60 > 56, so **5/8 > 7/12**
3. 2/3 vs. 8/12: 2/3 = 8/12, so **they are equal**
4. 7/9 vs. 5/6: Cross multiply: 7*6=42, 9*5=45. 42 < 45, so **7/9 < 5/6**
5. 11/15 vs. 3/4: Cross multiply: 11*4=44, 15*3=45. 44 < 45, so **11/15 < 3/4**

## Section I: Word Problems

1. **1 cup.** 2/3 * 3/2 = 6/6 = 1 cup of flour.

2. **3 3/8 feet.** 5 1/4 - 1 7/8 = 21/4 - 15/8 = 42/8 - 15/8 = 27/8 = 3 3/8 feet.

3. **1/12 of the trip.** Completed: 3/4 + 1/6 = 9/12 + 2/12 = 11/12.
   Remaining: 1 - 11/12 = 1/12.

4. **Capacity is approximately 42.86 liters (300/7 liters).**
   Let C = total capacity. (2/5)C + 15 = (3/4)C.
   15 = (3/4)C - (2/5)C = (15/20 - 8/20)C = (7/20)C.
   C = 15 * 20/7 = 300/7 liters.

5. **4/15.** First two pay: 1/3 + 2/5 = 5/15 + 6/15 = 11/15.
   Third friend: 1 - 11/15 = 4/15.

6. **$126.** After Monday: 120 - 120*(1/8) = 120 - 15 = 105.
   After Tuesday: 105 + 105*(1/5) = 105 + 21 = $126.
